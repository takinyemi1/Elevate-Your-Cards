The Idea Behind Elevate Your Cards


After this explanation, young adults, aged 18-30, will be able to understand "Elevate Your Cards."

Why the Name, "Elevate Your Cards"?

"Elevate Your Cards" is a blatant, straightforward, and easy to interpret slogan.

The name gives just enough away to let a possible consumer know what this service is, while also peaking their curiosity enough to make them wonder "How?".


If they decide to take a closer look at the logo, they would see that the logo has what can be considered as empty greeting cards.

From that, they would get the idea that "Elevate Your Cards" is a business that "elevates" your greeting cards.

Why Use "Elevate Your Cards"?

"Elevate Your Cards" is used to show the recipient of the greeting card that they're appreciated, even more if the option to add a specialized message is chosen.



It would allow the recipient to show that they are paid attention to.

The card has a possibility to include pictures and designs related to the recipient's interests; pictures and designs that wouldn't be known unless they were listened to.

Now, young adults aged 18-30, are able to understand "Elevate Your Cards".
