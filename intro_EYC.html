About Elevate Your Cards


We, young adults aged 18-30, tend to just choose one of the first few cards we see on a card stand, not putting thought into the message or design, because of how easily acessible they are.


"Elevate Your Cards" is a cardmaking business that services young adults aged 18-30 by elevating the cards they gift for special events, to make the recipient(s) feel more prioritized and appreciated.


Each section on the navigation bar will lead you to learning more in depth about what "Elevate Your Cards" card designs will look like, what the purpose of it is, and instruct you on how to use the service.
