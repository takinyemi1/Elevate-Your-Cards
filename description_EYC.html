Describe Elevate Your Cards


After this description, young adults aged 18-30, will be able to identify "Elevate Your Cards."

The Features & Design of Elevate Your Cards


The front of a card made by E.Y.C. will have large, noticeable lettering with a picture related to it either aligned to the left, aligned to the right, aligned to the top or the bottom, or drawn in between the words.

Written in the large, noticeable lettering will be a sort of slogan related to the recipient or a quote the recipient says often.

The actual design of the front of the card will either relate to the recipient's interests if specified,



OR

If the recipient's interests are not specified, the design of the card will be related to the month the card will be gifted on, or the season the card will be gifted in.

As for the inside design of the card, the same rules for the front design apply.


For the message inside, the person gifting the card will have an option to write a personalized message for the recipient in the request form, that will then be written inside of the card.

OR

If the person gifting the card opts out of writing a personalized message, a meaningful message will be generated from the interests of the recipient that were listed.

OR

If the person gifting the card was very scarce with the information that they submitted in the request form, a general message will generated based off of the month the card will be gifted on, or the season the card will be gifted in.
