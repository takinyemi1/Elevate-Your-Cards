How to Use Elevate Your Cards

After this instruction, young adults aged 18-30, will know how to request for a card to be made by "Elevate Your Cards".

Step One

Click this button or the tab that says "E.Y.C. Request Form" in order to begin the process for requesting a card to be made by "Elevate Your Cards".


DISCLAIMER: The following names are not real; they are just to be used as examples.

Step Two

Enter your first name as demonstrated.


Step Three

Enter your last name as demonstrated.


Step Four

Enter your email as demonstrated.


Step Five

Enter your phone number as demonstrated.


Step Six

DISCLAIMER: This option is only for if it is not possible for you to pick the card up in person.

Enter your address.


Step Seven

Enter the recipient's first name as demonstrated.


Step Eight

Enter the recipient's last name as demonstrated.


Step Nine

Enter the recipient's phone number as demonstrated (This is optional).


Step Ten

DISCLAIMER: This option is only for if you would like for the card to be sent directly to the recipient.

Enter the recipient's address.


Step Eleven

Enter the recipient's top five interests/hobbies (only three out of five are required) as demonstrated.






Step Twelve

Enter a personalized message for the recipient or N/A to opt out as demonstrated.


What Next?

Afterwards, your data will be sent to our database where it will be processed. The page will then be reloaded, where you'll be able to see the information you submitted, essentially "printed" on the screen. On the last line, you will see that your information was successfully added.




To receive the card, a message will be sent to both your phone number and email notifying you of the card being done and where to pick it up.

OR

If you do not have the ability to pick it up in-person, it can be delivered to your address.

Now, young adults aged 18-30 will know how to operate the website for "Elevate Your Cards" and request a form for a card to be made.
